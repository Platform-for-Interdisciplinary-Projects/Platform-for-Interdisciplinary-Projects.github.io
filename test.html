Hackathon

Hackathons are a chance to prove your skills in a team and in a short time frame.

We will organise our own Hackathon as a chance to bring together participants around practical sessions and develop chunks of our platform. Also we will participate in other hackathons to get to know people and test and enjoy our skills together.
