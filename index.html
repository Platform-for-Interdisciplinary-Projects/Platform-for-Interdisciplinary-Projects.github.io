Our Vision

We start with Workshop to inspire, spread the message, and we offer an entry point for future work. Our goal is an ultra low-cost academy that trains smart people to build with technology, empower them as problem solvers, and work on SDG projects in order to show and improve their skills. Our trainings in Vienna are meant to prepare our workshops in Iraq, Lebanon, Georgia, etc.

What motivates us to build a solution in the area of technology, education and innovation:

Code-it-forward

The best way to gain experience and test our learning model early.

We are working on code-it-forward, which we will offer in the MENA area in collaboration with our partner Living Light International.

In Austria we can perform 2-day workshop for young people to get to know programming.

In developing countries we can prototype our system in a few days, provide direct value, and give stakeholders a chance to get to know us and our way of working.

Trips

Part of our solution is building on top of existing solution and combining working concepts.

And of course it helps the team spirit if we go on trips and visit events together. Therefore we will visit some of the world’s most succesful initiatives to learn from and become inspired.

Sprints

Because we’re a non-profit of volunteers, showing consistent progress is important, but also adapting to people schedule and working distributed but coordinated is important. We work through GitHub and their issue management helps us participants to prioritize and assign issues to skills. Diverse skill sets are required, and so we give people from all stages of development a chance to develop their projects.

Hackathon

Hackathons are a chance to prove your skills in a team and in a short time frame.

We will organise our own Hackathon as a chance to bring together participants around practical sessions and develop chunks of our platform. Also we will participate in other hackathons to get to know people and test and enjoy our skills together.
