Hackathon 01 - on Sunday 02.02.2020


Hackathons are a chance to prove your skills in a team and in a short time frame,12 hours for projects and learning. Try out new technologies, collaborate with other developers, focus for 12 hours straight, design / build around meaningful challenges. We will organise our own Hackathon as a chance to bring together participants around practical sessions and develop chunks of our platform. Also we will participate in other hackathons to get to know people and test and enjoy our skills together.

People invited are coming from: 20% data science, 30% developers, 50% others You can invite friends or share a link to our Eventbrite page.




  • Professionals
  • Learners
  • Complete beginners
  • Students

  • We’ll start with a short impromptu conference, meaning whoever wants to can announce to have a 1-minute pitch, and a 20-minute session.
  • We will prepare more than 7 challenges, but you can also bring your own projects and focus on it for 12-hours.
  • 3 challenge themes (around SDG’s)
  • Time schedule: 10:00-22:30
  • Jury Awards three prices. (RaspberryPi kit, ticket to conference, certificate)
  • Up to 40 participants
  • WU Gründungszentrum
  • Lunch and dinner and snacks and drinks
